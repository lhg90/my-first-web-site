Recent Carrer

Season 12

On February 18, 2022, Lee played his 1,000th career game against KT Rolster. He was the second player to achieve the milestone. He locked in Leblanc and won the game.

On February 20, 2022, Lee played his 700th LCK game. He is the first to achieve the 700 game mark in the LCK. The game was against NS RedForce in which he locked in Ahri for the game.

During the 2022 LCK Spring Split, Lee was a member of the team that went undefeated in the regular season with an 18-0 record, becoming the first team to achieve this record since the leagueâ€™s inception. In the playoffs, Lee achieved his 10th LCK title, defeating Gen.G in the finals 3-1.

On July 8, 2022, Lee became the first player to achieve 500 wins in the LCK. The match was against Gen.G.

Lee became the first player to play 100 games in World Championship. He also surpassed Uzi and became the player with most kills in World Championship.

On November 28, 2022, T1 announced that Lee had re-signed on a three-year contract.

Season 13

On January 20, Lee overtook Gorilla and became the player with the most assist in the LCK in the game against KT Rolster. He also became the first player to have reached 2,700 kills in the LCK on the same day.
